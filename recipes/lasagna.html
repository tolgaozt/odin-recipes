Lasagna

We believe some things in life are worth taking a little time over, and lasagne is definitely one of them. It's one of the world’s most beloved meals, and for good reason; it’s tasty, rich, comforting soul food. This recipe makes a huge lasagne, so it’s great for a crowd – or make two smaller ones and freeze one.

Ingredients:

  • 3 Tbsp. tomato paste
  • 1-1/2 cups sour cream, divided
  • 1/2 cup KRAFT Greek Vinaigrette Dressing, divided
  • 2 tsp. each garlic powder, ground coriander and ground cumin, divided
  • 2 lb. boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 12 pita breads
  • 1 pkg. (7 oz.) hummus
  • 1 large tomato, cut into 12 slices
  • 1 pkg. (4 oz.) crumbled feta cheese

    • Cheese Sauce:

  • 100g butter
  • 1/2 cup white flour
  • 3.5 cups milk
  • 2 cups grated tasty cheddar cheese
  • 1 Tbsp Dijon mustard

Recipe Steps

  1. Mix tomato paste with 1 cup sour cream; 1/4 cup dressing; and 1-1/2 tsp. each garlic powder, coriander and cumin until blended. Pour over chicken in shallow dish; turn to evenly coat both sides of each thigh with sour cream mixture. Refrigerate 3 hours to marinate.
  2. Heat oven to 400°F. Cover rimmed baking sheet with foil; spray with cooking spray. Place 2 large skewers, parallel to each other and 1-1/2 inches apart, on prepared baking sheet. Repeat with 2 additional skewers.
  3. Remove chicken from marinade; discard marinade. Fold chicken thighs in half, then thread evenly onto skewers, tightly threading half the thighs onto each set of parallel skewers.
  4. Drizzle with 2 Tbsp. of the remaining dressing.
  5. Bake 30 to 35 min. or until chicken is done (165°F). Remove chicken from oven. Heat broiler.
  6. Broil chicken, 4 inches from heat, 5 min. on each side or until golden brown on both sides. Meanwhile, mix remaining sour cream, dressing and seasonings until blended. Reserve for later use.
  7. Stand 1 kebab on work surface, then use sharp knife to cut chicken vertically, from top to bottom, into thin strips.
  8. Stack pita breads on microwaveable plate; cover with damp paper towel. Microwave on HIGH 30 sec.
  9. Spread pita breads with hummus; top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese and chicken. Drizzle with reserved sour cream mixture; roll up.

Now to the Cheese Sauce!


  1. Melt butter in a large saucepan over a medium-low heat.
  2. Add flour and cook, stirring, for 3-5 minutes (don’t let it brown).
  3. Remove from heat and slowly add all the warmed milk in a thin stream, whisking all the time, until smooth.
  4. Return to heat and cook, stirring, until thickened.
  5. Add the cheese and mustard, stirring until smooth.
  6. Season to taste and remove from heat.

Voila ! You've got a perfect Lasagna ready for 4 people!
Prepare the table and get ready to enjoy your meal!
